Awareness Programme (CAP) during a Disaster

What is CAP?

CAP is a programme that aims to educate people about disasters and how to prepare for them. It covers a wide range of topics, including evacuation procedures, first aid, and emergency supplies.

Why is CAP important?

CAP is important because it helps people to be better prepared for disasters. This can reduce the risk of injury and death, and can also help to minimize damage to property.

How can I get involved in CAP?

There are many ways to get involved in CAP, including attending training sessions, volunteering for CAP events, and spreading the word about CAP in your community.
