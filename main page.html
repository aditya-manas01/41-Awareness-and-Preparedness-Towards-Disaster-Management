About Disaster Management

Disasters can strike anytime, anywhere, and can have devastating effects on individuals, families, communities, and entire countries. Therefore, it's crucial to have awareness and preparedness towards disaster management.

Mock Exercises

  • School Safety Programmes (SSP)
  • Awareness Programme (CAP)

These exercises are aimed at enhancing the preparedness and response capabilities of our community to deal with any emergency or disaster situation. The exercises are designed to simulate real-life scenarios and test the response capabilities of various agencies involved in disaster management.
